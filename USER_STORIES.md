# User Stories

> Version: 0.1
>
> Status: Draft
>
> Purpose:
>
> This document captures the primary user goals that drive the design and development of LVN Atlas.
> Every feature, API, and business rule should ultimately support one or more of these user stories.

---

# Epic 1 — Account & Profile

---

## Story US-001

**As a person planning to relocate,**

I want to create an account,

so that I can securely save and manage my migration plans.

---

## Story US-002

**As a user,**

I want to maintain my personal profile,

so that recommendations are personalized according to my situation.

---

## Story US-003

**As a returning user,**

I want to log in securely,

so that I can continue working on my migration plans.

---

# Epic 2 — Migration Planning

---

## Story US-004

**As a migrant,**

I want to create a migration blueprint,

so that I can estimate the financial impact of relocating.

---

## Story US-005

**As a migrant,**

I want to compare multiple destination cities,

so that I can make a better relocation decision.

---

## Story US-006

**As a user,**

I want to generate a personalized monthly budget,

so that I understand my expected living expenses before relocating.

---

## Story US-007

**As a user,**

I want to edit my estimated expenses,

so that my migration blueprint reflects my personal lifestyle.

---

# Epic 3 — Recommendations

---

## Story US-008

**As a user,**

I want to see migration journeys from people similar to me,

so that I can make decisions using real-world experiences.

---

## Story US-009

**As a user,**

I want to understand why recommendations were made,

so that I can trust the generated migration blueprint.

---

## Story US-010

**As a user,**

I want to receive an Atlas Score,

so that I know whether relocating is financially sustainable.

---

# Epic 4 — Planning & Decision Making

---

## Story US-011

**As a user,**

I want to save multiple migration blueprints,

so that I can compare different cities, salaries, or lifestyles.

---

## Story US-012

**As a user,**

I want to revisit my saved plans later,

so that I can update them whenever my circumstances change.

---

# Epic 5 — Community Intelligence

---

## Story US-013

**As an experienced migrant,**

I want to anonymously contribute my monthly expenses,

so that future migrants can benefit from real migration experiences.

---

## Story US-014

**As a contributor,**

I want my personal identity to remain anonymous,

so that I can safely share financial information.

---

# Epic 6 — Administration

---

## Story US-015

**As a platform administrator,**

I want to review submitted migration journeys,

so that inaccurate or abusive data does not affect recommendations.

---

## Story US-016

**As an administrator,**

I want to manage cities, categories, and platform data,

so that recommendations remain accurate and up to date.

---

# Future User Stories

The following stories are intentionally excluded from the MVP and will be evaluated in future releases.

- AI relocation assistant
- Community discussions
- Apartment recommendations
- Loan planning
- Insurance planning
- Employer relocation support
- International migration support
