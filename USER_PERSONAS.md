# User Personas

> Version: 0.1
>
> Purpose:
>
> This document defines the primary users of LVN Atlas.
> Every feature, API, database table, and business decision should ultimately solve a real problem experienced by one or more of these users.

---

# Persona 1 — Hitesh

## Background

- Age: 26
- Occupation: Software Developer
- Current City: Udaipur, Rajasthan
- Destination: Mumbai
- Monthly Salary: ₹55,000
- Family: Parents live in Rajasthan
- Lifestyle: Vegetarian
- Transportation: Public Transport
- Current Housing: Own House
- Future Housing: Rented Apartment

---

## Goal

Determine whether relocating to Mumbai is financially sustainable while continuing to save money every month.

---

## Questions

- Can I actually afford Mumbai?
- How much should I expect to spend every month?
- Which expenses am I forgetting?
- Can I bring my parents later?
- Should I rent alone or share a flat?

---

## Biggest Fear

Moving to a new city only to realize that the higher salary does not improve overall quality of life.

---

## Success

Creates a realistic migration plan and relocates with confidence.

---

# Persona 2 — Ameer

## Background

- Age: 29
- Occupation: Software Engineer
- Current City: Mumbai
- Originally From: Rajasthan
- Monthly Salary: ₹65,000
- Family: Lives Alone
- Experience: Already relocated successfully

---

## Goal

Help future migrants by anonymously sharing his monthly expenses.

---

## Questions

- Can my experience help someone similar?
- Can I update my expenses as prices change?
- Is my data anonymous?

---

## Biggest Fear

Sharing personal financial information publicly.

---

## Success

Contributes anonymous migration data that improves recommendations for future users.

# Persona 3 — Rishabh

## Background

- Age: 31
- Occupation: Sales Executive
- Current City: Lucknow
- Destination: Mumbai
- Family: Wife + Child
- Monthly Salary: ₹62,000

---

## Goal

Determine whether relocating with his family immediately is financially realistic.

---

## Questions

---

## Success
